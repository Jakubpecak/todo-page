Welcome to Todo Website!

This page has been specially designed for you. Share it effortlessly and manage your tasks with ease. Begin by adding your first task and embark on a more organized journey!

Here are some additional features at your disposal to help you make the most of this task management tool:

  • Add tasks: Easily add new tasks to your list by clicking the "Add Task" button. You can give each task a title, description, and due date.
  • Remove tasks: If you've completed a task or no longer need it, you can delete it from your list with a simple click.
  • Edit tasks: Need to make changes to a task? No problem! You can edit the task title, description, or due date to keep your list up to date.
